Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Deep Reload the Safari extension. Deep Reload is designed to help you reload pages or page elements while bypassing cache. The app does not require account registration and is not intended to collect personal information from users.

Information We Collect

Deep Reload does not intentionally collect, store, or sell personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment information.

The extension stores its configuration settings locally in your browser using extension storage so your preferences can persist between sessions.

How the App Uses Browser Access

To provide its functionality, the Safari extension may access the currently loaded page, page elements, and related browser tab context. This access is used only to enable features such as page reload, element-aware reload actions, visual highlighting, notifications, and settings behavior.

Based on the current implementation, this information is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to a developer-controlled server.

Analytics and Third-Party Services

Deep Reload does not currently include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or external tracking technologies in the app or extension.

If you download the app through the App Store, Apple may process certain data as part of providing the store, licensing, purchases, or device services. That processing is governed by Apple’s own policies and terms.

Data Retention

Any settings saved by Deep Reload remain on your device until you change them, clear browser extension storage, or remove the app and extension.

Your Choices

  • You can disable the Safari extension at any time in Safari settings.
  • You can remove the app from your Mac to stop all app-related processing.
  • You can reset extension settings by clearing the extension’s locally stored data.

Children’s Privacy

Deep Reload is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
